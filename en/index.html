(01) — Ethos

A home doesn't have to be complicated. It has to feel right.

Your home is designed around who you are. A place that feels warm, that feels right — in full harmony with the people who live there. Nothing more. Nothing less.

Whether you are reworking a single room or planning a complete project: your story comes first. You work with one interior architect, driven by honest, raw materials that breathe, age and grow more beautiful over time.

Everything begins with you — with how you live, how you move through your space in the morning, what you truly need. That is what is listened to. What follows is an interior that feels personal, stays timeless and is made to last — a home you come home to.

8. Cookies & analytics

This website uses cookies and similar technologies. On your first visit, a cookie notice asks for your choice. Only after you click “Accept” are analytics and advertising cookies placed; if you choose “Decline”, none of these are loaded.

We use the following services:

  • Google Analytics 4 — to understand, in anonymised form, how visitors use the website (pages visited, duration, source). IP addresses are anonymised.
  • Google Ads — to measure the return of any advertising campaigns, for example whether a contact enquiry originated from an advertisement.

These services are provided by Google. In doing so, data may be processed outside the European Union. We use Google Consent Mode, so that no tracking starts without your consent. You can withdraw or change your choice at any time by clearing your browser cookies, after which the cookie notice will appear again.

Functional data strictly necessary to make the website work is not subject to this consent.
